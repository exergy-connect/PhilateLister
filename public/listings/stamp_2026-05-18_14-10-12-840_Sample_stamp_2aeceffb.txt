This is a genuine **Sweden 1874 12 öre Blue Official stamp (Tjenstefrimärke)**, identifiable by its denomination and the "TJENSTEFRIMÄRKE" inscription. Based on the 1878 cancellation date, this belongs to the first series of official stamps issued between 1874 and 1881.

Sweden Scott #O6 Used 1874 12ö Blue Official Stamp - ÅSBO 1878 CDS Cancel

**Catalog Identification:**

**Condition Report:**
*   **Overall Grade:** Used, with a clear and attractive cancellation.
*   **Centering:** Poorly centered, shifted significantly toward the upper-right corner. The left and bottom margins are very wide, while the top and right perforations touch or slightly overlap the design frame.
*   **Perforations:** Generally intact with approximately 14 horizontal and 17 vertical perforation teeth visible. There is some minor irregular wear typical for the era, but no significant missing teeth are noted.
*   **Paper/Color:** The blue color remains distinct. There is noticeable toning and foxing/staining on the left side of the stamp and some general light soiling consistent with postal use.

**Cancellation Details:**
*   **City:** ÅSBO
*   **Date:** 18 November 1878 ("18 11 78")
*   **Historical Note:** November 18, 1878, fell on a **Monday**. The Åsbo parish (located in Östergötland) was a rural agricultural community during this time, with a population of approximately 2,500 people. A post office was established there in the mid-19th century to serve the local farming population.
