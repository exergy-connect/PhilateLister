**Identification & Catalog Number:**
Sweden Scott O6 (12 öre, blue). This is a "Tjenstefrimärke" (Official stamp) featuring the coat of arms design, typically issued between 1874 and 1889.

**Condition Report:**
This is a used stamp with a heavy, distinct cancellation. Centering is shifted slightly toward the top right. The paper shows normal signs of age with a faint ink smudge on the left margin edge. The bottom left corner is distinctly blunted with missing/shortened perforations. 

**Perforation Details:**
*   **Top:** Approximately 13 horizontal dents
*   **Bottom:** Approximately 13 horizontal dents
*   **Left:** Approximately 10 vertical dents (noticeably worn/missing at the bottom corner)
*   **Right:** Approximately 11 vertical dents 

**Cancellation Details:**
The stamp features a prominent, very readable circular datestamp (CDS) from **ÅSBO**. The date at the bottom of the cancel reads **18 11 78**, indicating November 18, 1878. 
*Historical note: November 18, 1878, fell on a Monday.*
