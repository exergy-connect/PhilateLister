### **Listing Body**
Up for auction is a fine example of the Netherlands 1872-1891 definitive issue. This 15-cent stamp features the portrait of **King William III** (Willem III), who served as King of the Netherlands from 1849 until his death in 1890.

The highlight of this specific stamp is the exceptionally crisp and full circular date stamp from **Zuidbroek**. Cancelled on June 12, 1882, the strikes are bold and provide a perfect snapshot of Dutch postal history from over 140 years ago. Whether you are completing a specialized Netherlands collection or look for clear town cancels, this is a beautiful specimen to add to your album.
