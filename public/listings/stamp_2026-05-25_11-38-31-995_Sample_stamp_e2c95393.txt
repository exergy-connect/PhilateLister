**Sweden Official Scott O4 12ö Blue Used - Crisp ÅSBO 18.11.1878 Date Cancel**

**Key Facts**
* **Country:** Sweden
* **Denomination:** 12 öre
* **Issue:** 1874-1884 "Tjenstefrimärke" (Official/Service Stamp) 
* **Scott Number:** Scott #O4
* **Cancel:** Bold Circular Date Stamp (CDS) from ÅSBO, dated Nov 18, 1878.
* **Condition:** Used; off-center to the right.

**Condition**
Used with centering off-right, featuring a remarkably clear and bold 19th-century town cancel.

**Description**
An attractive example of the 1874 Swedish 12 öre blue official stamp, depicting the lesser coat of arms. This particular specimen is distinguished by its exceptionally crisp and legible "ÅSBO" circular date stamp. The strike is remarkably bold for the era, clearly showing the date of November 18, 1878. 

The stamp retains bright, fresh blue color and good paper integrity. While the centering is pulled to the right, the overall presentation is excellent for a collector of classic Swedish postal history or town cancels. A very clean and well-preserved piece from the early years of Sweden's official mail service.
