### **Item Description**

For your consideration is one of the most legendary rarities in United States philately: the **1918 24¢ Curtiss Jenny Airmail Invert (Scott #C3a)**.

This iconic stamp features the "Jenny" biplane printed in blue, famously inverted within its carmine-rose frame. This error occurred during the very first production run of U.S. airmail stamps when a single sheet of 100 was accidentally sold to a collector at a post office in Washington, D.C.
