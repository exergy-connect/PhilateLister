### **Cancel Information**
*   **Type:** Three-ring numeral cancel.
*   **Numeral:** **2**
*   **City:** Numeral "2" was assigned to **Helsingør** (known in English as **Elsinore**).
*   **Historical Context:** Helsingør is a historic port city located at the narrowest point of the Øresund strait. Around the time this stamp was in use (c. 1875–1885), the city had a population of approximately **9,000 people**. It was a crucial administrative and maritime hub, famous globally as the setting for William Shakespeare's *Hamlet* at Kronborg Castle.
