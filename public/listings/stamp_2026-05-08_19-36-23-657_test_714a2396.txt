**Denmark 20ø Gray & Rose - Numeral "51" Concentric Ring Cancel**

**Key Facts**
*   **Country:** Denmark
*   **Issue:** Bi-color Arms/Numeral Issue (c. 1875-1885)
*   **Denomination:** 20 øre
*   **Color:** Gray center with Rose frame
*   **Cancel:** Numeral "51" within a four-ring concentric circle

**Condition**
Sound used condition with typical centering for the era and clean perforations.

**Description**
A handsome example of Denmark’s classic 19th-century bi-color series. This 20 øre value displays a sharp contrast between the gray central numeral and the intricate rose-colored frame. The stamp is characterized by a clear strike of a numeral cancel (51) in the traditional concentric ring style. With its bright colors and overall clean presentation, this is a great addition for any specialist in Scandinavian philately or numeral cancels.
