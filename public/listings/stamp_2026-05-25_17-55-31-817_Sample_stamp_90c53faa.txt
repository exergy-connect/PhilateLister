### Cancellation Details
*   **Type:** Circular Date Stamp (CDS)
*   **Town:** **ÅSBO**
*   **Date:** November 12, 1878 (**12 / 18 11 78**)
    *   *Note on Swedish Format:* Per the standard of the era, the central digit "12" represents the day of the month. The bottom line splits the year around the month: "18" (century) "11" (month) "78" (year). 
    *   **Weekday Check:** November 12, 1878, was a **Tuesday**, which is a standard business day for postal operations. 
*   **Town History:** Åsbo was a rural parish in the Boxholm municipality of Östergötland County. In the late 1870s, it was a small agricultural community with a population of approximately 1,600 residents. Such clear cancels from smaller rural stations are often desirable for "Socken" (parish) or town-mark collectors.
