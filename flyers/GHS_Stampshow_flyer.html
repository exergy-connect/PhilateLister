✦ USPS FIRST DAY OF ISSUE: Winter Wreath ✦

Global Forever stamp

Released Friday 18th at 11:00 AM

A snowy holiday wreath for the season’s Global Forever issue.

Photographer Natalia Kostikova built the wreath from artificial branches and craft-store materials, painting each ornament and dusting the greenery with baking soda for frost. Designed by Greg Breeding for the United States Postal Service.

Mark your calendar: the Greater Houston Stamp Show returns September eighteenth through twentieth at the Humble Civic Center — three days you will not want to miss. This is Houston’s gathering place for collectors, dealers, and anyone curious about the stories stamps tell. And here is what makes this year unforgettable: on Friday at eleven, the United States Postal Service holds a First Day of Issue ceremony for the Winter Wreath Global Forever stamp — a snowy holiday design you can witness at the moment it enters the mailstream. Then bid in the GHSS donation silent auction, anchored by a classic-era highlight: United States Scott number ninety-one, the fifteen-cent Lincoln with E-Grill. Come for the stamps. Stay for an exclusive experience you will only find here.
