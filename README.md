# 📮 PhilateLister: Your Virtual Stamp Shop Assistant

**The easiest way to get your stamps out of the stockbook and onto eBay.**

PhilateLister is a digital assistant designed specifically for stamp dealers. If you can take a digital photo and upload a file, you can use this tool to list your inventory in a fraction of the time it takes to type it out by hand.

## How It Helps You

- **Identifies for you** — Our “AI Vision” acts like a digital magnifying glass. It looks at your stamp, recognizes the country and design, and suggests the likely year and issue.

- **Writes the “sales talk”** — It drafts a professional, polite eBay description—including historical context and technical details—so you don’t have to stare at a blank screen.

- **No technical headache** — Once it is set up, you don’t “run” software. You simply put your photos in a folder, and the system handles the “thinking” in the background.

- **Expert knowledge** — It is trained on millions of historical documents, helping you spot the difference between a common issue and a potentially valuable variety.

- **You stay in charge** — You set the target price, and you review the draft description for accuracy before you finalize. PhilateLister assists; it does not replace your judgment as the dealer.

## The “3-Step” Process

1. **Snap** — Take a clear photo of your stamp on a black background.

2. **Upload** — Upload the photo to PhilateLister, and optionally provide hints or aspects you would like to highlight. Set your target price and submit for drafting.

3. **Review** — Check the description for accuracy and modify as desired, then click to upload to eBay for listing.
