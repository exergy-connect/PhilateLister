**Denmark Scott #31 20ø Bicolor, Gray & Rose - VF Numeral 152 Cancel**

**Key Facts**
* **Country:** Denmark
* **Scott Number:** 31
* **Denomination:** 20 Øre
* **Colors:** Gray frame with Rose center
* **Cancel:** Numeral "152" in concentric rings
* **Period:** 1875 Bicolor Issue

**Condition**
Very Fine used with sound perforations and a crisp, bold numeral strike.

**Description**
An exceptionally attractive example of Denmark’s classic Bicolor series. This 20 øre value (Scott #31) showcases vibrant, fresh colors and sharp printing detail. The standout feature of this specific piece is the "152" numeral cancel, which has been struck with superb clarity, centered beautifully over the design. 

This specimen is identified as a 4th printing variety with an inverted watermark. The stamp is well-centered for this challenging issue and possesses great "eye appeal." It is a choice selection for the collector of Scandinavian classics or those specializing in Danish numeral cancellations.
