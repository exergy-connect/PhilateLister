*   **Denomination:** 8 Øre

### **Cancel Identification**
*   **Type:** Numeral cancel in three concentric rings.
*   **Numeral:** **21**
*   **Location:** **Kolding**
*   **Confidence Level:** High. The digits "2" and "1" are clearly visible. In the Danish postal system of this era, numeral 21 was assigned to the town of Kolding, an important seaport and market town in Jutland. 
*   **Chronology:** Numeral cancels were introduced in 1851 and remained in use through the bicolored era, making this a perfectly period-appropriate usage.
